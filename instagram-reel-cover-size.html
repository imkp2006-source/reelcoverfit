Recommended workflow

  1. Design on a 9:16 canvas.
  2. Keep key text, faces, and logos near the center.

  4. Check the safe zone, profile crop, Explore preview, and thumbnail analysis.

Why one preview is not enough

A full-screen vertical cover can be cropped when shown in a grid or compact discovery surface. Use the synchronized previews to check the same design in multiple contexts.
