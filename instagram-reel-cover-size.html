Best Instagram Reel cover size

Use a vertical 9:16 image for your Reel cover. The most common export size is 1080 pixels wide by 1920 pixels tall. This gives you a sharp vertical cover that matches the shape of Reels.

Quick size checklist

  • Aspect ratio: 9:16 vertical
  • Common pixel size: 1080 × 1920 px
  • Best placement: keep title, face, product, and logo near the center
  • Profile grid: check the center square crop before posting
  • File quality: export a clear JPG, PNG, or WebP image

What is the Reel cover safe zone?

The safe zone is the center area where important elements are less likely to feel cut off or hidden. Even if your image is 9:16, text near the top, bottom, or extreme edges can look bad in previews. Keep your title, face, logo, and product photo closer to the center.

Why profile grid crop matters

Your Reel cover may appear as a vertical image in one place and as a square-like crop on your profile grid. This is why a cover can look good in full view but poor on your profile. ReelCoverFit includes a profile grid crop preview so you can check the center area before posting.

Recommended workflow

  1. Design your cover in Canva, Photoshop, Figma, or any editor.
  2. Export it as a 1080 × 1920 px vertical image.
  3. Upload it to ReelCoverFit.
  4. Check the safe zone and profile grid crop.
  5. Adjust your design if important text or faces are too close to the edges.
Tip: Keep short titles and faces in the center. Avoid placing tiny text at the bottom of the cover.

FAQ

Is 1080 × 1920 required?

It is not the only possible size, but it is a simple high-quality choice because it follows the 9:16 vertical format.

Can I use this tool after designing in Canva?

Yes. Download your design from Canva and upload it to ReelCoverFit to check whether it fits correctly.

Does the tool upload my image?

No. The image preview is generated locally in your browser.
