Why Is My Instagram Reel Cover Cropped? How to Fix It

Your Reel cover can get cropped because Instagram may show it differently in the full Reel view, profile grid, and thumbnail previews. The fix is to use a 9:16 image and keep important content inside the safe center area.

Common reasons your Reel cover gets cropped

  • Your image is not close to the 9:16 vertical ratio.
  • Your title text is too close to the top or bottom edge.
  • The main subject is outside the center area.
  • The cover looks good in full view but bad in the profile grid crop.
  • The design has too much small text near the edges.

How to fix a cropped Reel cover

  1. Start with a 9:16 vertical canvas.
  2. Use 1080 × 1920 px for a clean export.
  3. Move your title, face, logo, or product toward the center.
  4. Leave space at the top and bottom.
  5. Check the profile grid crop before publishing.

Where should text be placed?

Place important text in the center area instead of the extreme top or bottom. Short, bold titles are easier to read than long sentences. If the cover has a face, keep the face and title balanced in the safe zone.

Why does the profile grid crop look different?

Vertical content often gets shown in a smaller grid preview. This can hide parts of the image that looked fine in the full 9:16 view. ReelCoverFit shows a center crop preview so you can see whether the main subject remains visible.

Best fix: Design in 9:16, keep the main subject centered, and preview the cover before uploading to Instagram.

FAQ

Can I stop all cropping completely?

You cannot control every preview style, but you can reduce the risk by using a 9:16 image and keeping important elements inside the safe center area.

Why is my title cut off?

Your title may be too close to the top, bottom, or side edge. Move the title toward the center and check it with the safe zone preview.

Does ReelCoverFit fix the image automatically?

ReelCoverFit currently helps you check and preview the cover. Use the preview to adjust your design in your editor, then test it again.
