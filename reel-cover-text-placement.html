Text placement checklist

  • Use short titles.
  • Keep text away from the top and bottom edges.
  • Maintain strong contrast against the background.
  • Check profile-grid and Explore previews.
  • Avoid placing essential words across a likely crop boundary.

Use the rule of thirds

The rule-of-thirds overlay can help balance text and a face or product while keeping both close to the center.
