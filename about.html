About ReelCoverFit

ReelCoverFit is a free online tool built to help creators check Instagram Reel cover size, safe zones, and profile grid crop before posting.

Many creators design a Reel cover that looks good while editing, but after posting, the text, face, logo, or title may look cropped or badly placed in different Instagram previews. ReelCoverFit helps reduce that guesswork by showing a simple 9:16 preview, safe zone overlay, and profile grid crop preview.

Why ReelCoverFit was created

Reel covers are important because they create the first impression of your content. A clean cover can make your profile look more organized and help viewers understand your Reel before opening it. ReelCoverFit was created as a simple, fast, and privacy-friendly way to check your cover before posting.

What the tool does

  • Checks your uploaded image dimensions
  • Compares the image with the recommended 9:16 Reel format
  • Shows a vertical Reel cover preview
  • Highlights safe and unsafe areas
  • Shows a profile grid crop preview
  • Lets you download a checked preview image

Privacy-first design

ReelCoverFit processes your image directly inside your browser. Your image is not uploaded to any server, and you do not need to create an account to use the tool.
