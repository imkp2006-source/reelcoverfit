Recommended format

Instagram Reel cover size guide

A vertical 9:16 image works best for Reel covers. A common high-quality size is 1080 × 1920 pixels.

Keep your important text, face, and logo near the center area. Top and bottom areas can be affected by app interface elements, captions, or preview crops.

Quick recommendation

  • Use vertical 9:16 format
  • Prefer 1080 × 1920 px
  • Keep text inside the safe zone
  • Check profile grid crop before posting
  • Avoid placing logos at extreme edges

Placement matters

Safe zone guide

ReelCoverFit highlights practical unsafe areas so you can avoid placing important elements too close to the edges.

Top area

Avoid placing important text very close to the top because it may feel cramped in previews.

Bottom area

Captions, buttons, or interface elements can make bottom text harder to read.

Center area

The center is usually safest for faces, titles, and logos.

Questions

FAQ

What is the best Instagram Reel cover size?

A 9:16 vertical image is recommended. A commonly used size is 1080 × 1920 pixels.

Does ReelCoverFit upload my image?

No. Your image is processed locally inside your browser. It is not sent to any server.

Why does my Reel cover get cropped?

Your cover can appear differently in the Reel view, profile grid, and previews. That is why checking safe zones helps.

Can I use this for YouTube Shorts covers?

You can use the 9:16 preview as a rough check, but this tool is currently focused on Instagram Reel covers.

Is ReelCoverFit official?

No. ReelCoverFit is an unofficial tool and is not affiliated with Instagram, Meta, or any social media platform.
