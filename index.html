Common questions about Instagram Reel cover size, safe zones, cropping, and privacy.

What is the best Instagram Reel cover size?

The best Instagram Reel cover size is a vertical 9:16 image. A commonly used high-quality size is 1080 × 1920 pixels.

What does ReelCoverFit check?

ReelCoverFit checks your uploaded Reel cover image dimensions, compares the aspect ratio with 9:16, shows a vertical Reel preview, highlights safe zones, and shows a profile grid crop preview.

Why does my Instagram Reel cover get cropped?

Your Reel cover can appear differently in the full Reel view, profile grid, and thumbnail previews. If your image is not close to 9:16, or if text is placed near the edges, parts of the cover may look cropped or hard to read.

What is a Reel cover safe zone?

A Reel cover safe zone is the area where important elements like text, faces, logos, and titles are less likely to be cut off or hidden. Keeping important content near the center usually gives a cleaner preview.

How do I stop my Reel cover text from getting cut?

Use a 9:16 image and keep your main title, face, logo, or product away from the top, bottom, and extreme side edges. ReelCoverFit helps you preview these safe and unsafe areas before posting.
